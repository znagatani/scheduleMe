Photo to Shift Conversion

Schedule Me utilizes your phone's camera to work its magic. Just open the app, snap a picture of your schedule (or upload an existing one), and Schedule Me will alert you 24 hours in advance of your next shift, and in any other time increment that you specify. Double check your upcoming shifts in advance for accuracy... or, with Schedule Me's 99.9% conversion accuracy... don't.

Cross-Platform

Schedule Me doesn't only work on your smartphone. It will automatically create shift-events for every device that you enable, for as many devices as you want. Schedule Me can even send you reminders via Facebook and Twitter! Just sync up your social media accounts and get ready to be your company's most reliable employee... there's a reason Schedule Me is your boss's best friend!
